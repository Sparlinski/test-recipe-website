Coconut Beef Curry





This creamy delicous curry is easy and simple to make. Curry powders are wide and varied in the dynamics they bring to a dish. If you have access to a local indian market, I highly recommend picking up a powder that you can get there, it will not only enhance the flavor of the dish but you can also bring a level of heat to the dish that you cannot get from standard store bought curry powders. Regardless, the spices and flavors that are combined here will be unleashed regardless of the powder you choose.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of cubed steak
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 pound of sweet potato
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 tablespoon of cinnamon powder
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 sprig of curry leaves
  • 3 cloves of diced garlic
  • tablespoon of ginger
  • 3 tablespoons of curry powder
  • half a can of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of beef broth
  • salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1 tablspoon of flour
  • half tablespoon of coconut sugar
